About Upchar Diagnostic Center

Your trusted partner in healthcare diagnostics

Who We Are

Upchar Diagnostic Center is a leading diagnostic service provider specializing in home sample collection across Delhi NCR. We bridge the gap between quality healthcare diagnostics and convenience, bringing professional lab services right to your doorstep.

Founded with a vision to make healthcare accessible and convenient, we partner with NABL-certified laboratories to ensure accurate and reliable test results. Our team of trained phlebotomists and healthcare professionals is committed to providing you with the best diagnostic experience.

We understand that visiting a lab can be time-consuming and stressful. That's why we've made it our mission to bring the lab to you, making healthcare more accessible for everyone, especially the elderly, busy professionals, and those with mobility challenges.
