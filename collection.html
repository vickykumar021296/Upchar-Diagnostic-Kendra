How Home Collection Works

Simple 4-step process to get your tests done

1

Book Your Test

Call us, use WhatsApp, or fill out our online booking form. Provide your details and preferred time slot.

2

Confirmation

We'll confirm your appointment via call or SMS. Our team will reach out to verify your address and timing.

3

Home Visit

Our certified phlebotomist arrives at your doorstep at the scheduled time with all necessary equipment.

4

Sample Collection

Quick and painless sample collection following all safety protocols. Samples are properly labeled and transported.

What Happens Next?

After sample collection, your samples are safely transported to our NABL-certified partner laboratory. Test results are processed and delivered to you via email and SMS within the promised turnaround time. You can also access your reports online through our secure portal.

Important Information

Fasting Requirements

Some tests require fasting (8-12 hours). Our team will inform you about any specific requirements when you book.

Timing

We offer flexible timing slots from 7 AM to 8 PM, including weekends. Early morning slots are available for fasting tests.

Preparation

Please keep your Aadhaar card or any valid ID ready. For certain tests, you may need to follow specific preparation guidelines.

Payment

We accept cash, UPI, and card payments. Payment can be made at the time of sample collection or online during booking.
